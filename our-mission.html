Our Mission
Data Conversations is an open initiative to build a community of researchers, students, and practitioners to advance the Science of Data. The need for this has grown out of our data community privacy conversations with academicians and practitioners on need for a common platform to discuss, deliberate, cross pollinate and build together to advance the data centric technologies.
We roll this initiative out with the following mission:
Provide a platform to enable dialogue between different stakeholders on diverse data topics spanning the complete data lifecycle: collection, management, usage, governance, trust and monetization.
Build, nurture and mentor a community of researchers, practitioners and policy makers working to learn and expedite data centric innovations especially focused on newer areas like AI, Foundation Models, Edge, Meta-verse etc.
